Our Culture

Sri Lanka, an island nation with a cultural heritage as diverse as its landscapes.


Sri Lanka's cultural mosaic reflects the influences of Indian, Arab, Malay, and European civilizations, creating a unique blend that is distinctly its own. From the ancient kingdoms that have left their mark on architectural wonders to the rituals and festivals that punctuate the calendar, every aspect of Sri Lankan culture tells a story of resilience and adaptation.

Beyond the tangible aspects of culture, experience the legendary Sri Lankan hospitality. Whether you find yourself in a bustling market, a rural village, or an urban center, the warmth of the people will leave an indelible mark on your journey.

Explore timeless sanctuaries adorned with intricate carvings and soaring stupas. These ancient temples are living monuments, testifying to a spirituality that has thrived for centuries.

Uncover tales of ancient glory at Sri Lanka's historical sites. From the awe-inspiring Sigiriya Rock Fortress to the ancient city of Anuradhapura and the stone walls of Polonnaruwa, each site is a chapter in a stone-written history.

Step into a world of color, rhythm, and jubilation during Sri Lanka's traditional festivals. Whether it's the lively beats of the Kandy Esala Perahera or the spiritual fervor of Vesak.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is it safe to travel to Sri Lanka in 2023?

    Yes, Sri Lanka is generally considered safe for tourists. The country has implemented measures to enhance security, and popular tourist destinations maintain a welcoming atmosphere. It's always advisable to check the latest travel advisories before planning your trip.

  • What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka in 2023?

    The best time to visit is generally from December to March, during the dry season on the west and south coasts. However, the east coast is ideal from April to September. Be sure to plan according to your preferred activities and regions.

  • Do I need a visa to enter Sri Lanka in 2023?

    Yes, most visitors require a visa to enter Sri Lanka. The process is straightforward, and you can apply for an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) online before your trip. Ensure that your passport has at least six months' validity.

  • What vaccinations do I need before traveling to Sri Lanka in 2023?

    While no specific vaccinations are mandatory for entry, it's recommended to be up-to-date on routine vaccines. Consult with your healthcare provider for advice on additional vaccines, such as Hepatitis A and B, typhoid, and Japanese encephalitis, depending on your travel plans.

  • How is the transportation system in Sri Lanka?

    Sri Lanka has a well-developed transportation network. Taxis, tuk-tuks, and buses are common modes of local transport. Trains are scenic and offer a unique travel experience. In 2023, transportation infrastructure continues to improve, providing convenient options for exploring the country.

  • What are the must-visit attractions in Sri Lanka this year?

    Sri Lanka boasts a rich tapestry of attractions. Explore the ancient wonders of Sigiriya, the historic city of Kandy, and the beautiful beaches of Mirissa. Don't miss the cultural festivals and wildlife safaris in national parks. Each region has something unique to offer in 2023, promising a diverse and memorable experience.
